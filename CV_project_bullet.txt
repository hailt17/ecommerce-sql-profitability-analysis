E-commerce Profitability & Customer LTV Optimization using SQL Server

- Analyzed a global B2C e-commerce dataset with over 101K transactional records using SQL Server to diagnose margin dilution and identify key profitability constraints.
- Discovered that aggressive marketing markdowns cannibalized unit-economics, revealing that 26% to 27% of all orders ran at a net loss due to an excessive average discount rate of 45.5%.
- Formulated data-driven recommendations to enforce a 30% Hard-Cap promotion ceiling on low-margin segments (Corporate) and categories to restore sustainable product profit margins.
- Engineered an advanced Time-Series analysis to map monthly rolling Year-over-Year (YoY) revenue velocities, isolating a seasonal structural contraction in July 2023 (-4.83% YoY).
- Developed a comprehensive RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Clustering Model using NTILE(5) and implemented a Market Basket Analysis via asynchronous SELF JOINs to isolate churn-risk VIP cohorts and reveal cross-selling pairing triggers.
